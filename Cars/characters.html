Sally
Sally is a light blue 2002 Porsche 911 Carrera, with aqua eyes, and high wheels.
She is the proprietor of Radiator Springs' Cozy Cone and Wheel Wagon Motels and the apple of Lightning McQueen's eye.
  • Top Speed : 177 mph (284 km/h)
  • Zero - 60 mph : 4.9 seconds
  • Engine Type : Rear-engine 3596 cc flat-6
  • Horsepower : 320 at 6800 rpm
